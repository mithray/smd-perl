Phrasing Elements

To use strong tags put double ** or __ on each side of a word or phrase. To use em tags put single * or _ on each side of a word or phrase.

Headings h1-h6

Use between 1 and 6 # at the start of a line to generate a heading. Superscript with carrots. Subscript with tilds.

Critic Markup

Comment with curly parenthesis and angle brackets Use ins with plus del with minus, mark with mark
